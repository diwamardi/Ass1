Welcome to Crypto Maps India

This website is mainly based on crypto maps for Indians.

Here, you can check the map to see where crypto enthusiasts are available in India or around you.

Our goal is to help you connect with other crypto people and build a strong community.

Features

  • Near by locations
  • private chatting

Registration Process

  • email
  • password
  • 2fa

Steps by step guide

  1. Sign up using your email with a strong password
  2. Set up 2FA with your preferred app (eg:Aegis)
  3. Toggle the location as per you requirment or give a specific location
  4. Check the map on home screen for other users
  5. Click on your near by location and check for available users
  6. Get in contact with users using the chat option
